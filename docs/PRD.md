# V!BE Visa & Travel

# 1. Product Vision

V!BE Visa & Travel is a digital platform for visa support and travel consulting.

The platform helps clients quickly find the right visa service, understand the required documents, submit an application, and receive professional support.

Internal business processes are managed in Notion CRM.

---

# 2. Target Audience

Primary audience:

- Individuals applying for visas
- Families
- Tourists
- Business travelers
- Students

---

# 3. MVP Scope

Included:

- Landing page
- Services catalog
- Country pages
- Visa information
- Contact form
- Application form
- Notion CRM integration

Excluded:

- Online payments
- Customer account
- Manager dashboard
- Internal CRM
- Online booking system
